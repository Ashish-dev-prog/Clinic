Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about visual booking or token tracking? We have answers.

How does the live token queue tracking work?

expand_more

Once you book a slot, PawCare assigns you a digital token (e.g. #07). The confirmation screen shows how many patients are currently ahead of you, estimated wait times, and progress. The queue updates automatically as the doctor completes consultations.

Can I book appointments for both myself and my pet?

expand_more

Yes, absolutely! PawCare is a unified platform. You can toggle between Human Clinics and Pet Vets, and the booking details form will dynamically update to collect the correct diagnostic or veterinary details.

Is there an extra fee for visual booking?

expand_more

No. PawCare only charges a standard nominal GST and booking convenience fee (₹18.00) in addition to the doctor's standard consultation fee (₹200.00). You can choose to pay online or directly at the reception counter.

What should I do in case of a medical emergency?

expand_more

Click the red pulsing "EMERGENCY" button located in the navbar of any page. It will instantly calculate the nearest emergency hospital, dial vet dispatch networks, and provide direct phone links to secure immediate ER support.
